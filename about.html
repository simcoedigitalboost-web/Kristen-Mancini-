Get to Know Me

The person behind your mortgage

I believe buying a home should feel exciting—not overwhelming.

I'm Kristen Mancini, and I help families in Orillia, Barrie, and across Simcoe County navigate one of the biggest financial decisions of their lives.

Before getting into mortgages, I earned my Bachelor of Commerce from McMaster University. But honestly? What prepared me most for this work wasn't a classroom—it was becoming a mom. When you're responsible for your family's future, you see finances differently. You understand what's at stake.

That's why I don't just process paperwork. I take the time to understand your situation, explain your options in plain English, and make sure you're not leaving money on the table. Too many first-time buyers miss out on grants and programs simply because nobody told them they existed.

I'm here to change that.
